Step 1
Define the answer space
What are the min and max possible answer values?
e.g. Koko bananas: speed ∈ [1, max(piles)]
Step 2
Write isFeasible(X) clearly
Given answer X — can we achieve X or better?
e.g. isFeasible(speed) = can eat all piles in H hours?
Step 3
Verify monotonicity — increasing
Pick X where feasible=true. Verify X+1 also feasible.
e.g. Speed=4 works → Speed=5 also works ✓
Step 4
Verify monotonicity — decreasing
Pick X where feasible=false. Verify X-1 also false.
e.g. Speed=1 fails → Speed=0 also fails ✓
